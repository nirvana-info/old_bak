Vehicle Accessories

Vehicle accessories are products that mount to the vehicle. Items that are included in this category are Towing Mirrors, Transmission Coolers, Tow Bars, Splash Guards, and Tailgate Louvers & Nets.

Towing Mirrors
A Towing Mirror is used for extending your current mirror outward so you can see the sides of the trailer.

  • Slides over existing vehicle mirrors to provide added visibility when towing
  • Easy installation, no tools required - mounts in seconds
  • Specifically designed for most major OEM mirrors
  • Fully adjustable, does not obstruct existing mirrors

Transmission Coolers
Transmission Coolers are used to help your automatic transmission when towing or steep inclines.

  • Ultrasonically soldered for reliability
  • 100% pressure tested at 150 psi for quality assurance
  • High efficiency turbulators in every tube for maximum heat dissipation
  • 3/8" push-on fittings for quick and easy installation

Tow Bars
Tow Bars are used to tow a vehicle typically behind an RV or other large vehicle. Tow bar brackets are mounted to the towed vehicle for the Tow Bar to connect too.

Splash Guards
The Splash Guards are custom molded with any of the detailed applications on the back of the packaging.

  • Custom molded OE styling fit with detailed application information on back of package
  • All installation hardware included
  • 6 part numbers cover all passenger cars, minivans, trucks, vans and SUVs
  • See the full line Highland Catalog for application fit information
    NOTE: Only available in the U.S.A.

Louvered Tailgates and Nets
The Louvered Tailgates and Nets are great additions for people that tow with 5th Wheels or just want a custom look to their vehicle.

    The Louvered Tailgate is the perfect choice for fifth wheelers who want a custom fit for their vehicle. It offers a light weight, aerodynamic design that actually improves gas mileage. It is made with molded plastic and has a center handle design for one hand operation.
    Caution: Not designed to carry weight in the down position. Do not sit or step on.
