Fifth Wheels

A fifth wheel is a specially designed hitch with a king pin receiver that mounts over or forward of the rear axle of the vehicle. The hitch connects with the king pin on the fifth wheel trailer. Fifth wheel hitches are easy to operate and require little maintenance.

15K & 16K Fifth Wheel
Hidden Hitch offers these two styles of hitches that mount to a rail kit that is installed in the truck bed. Quick-easy installation, strong, reliable performance and easy removal in seconds allows full access to the truck bed for maximum versatility.

  • Two-jaw locking system
  • Fore-and-aft pivot
  • Locking Jaw handle
  • Vertically adjusts from 12-1.2" to 16-1.2"
  • 15,000 lbs. towing capacity
  • 3,750 lbs. pin weight capacity
  • Wide stance one-piece legs for improved stability
  • Requires Base Rail & Installation Kit (sold separately)
  • 5 year limited warranty

15K & 16K Sliders
Fifth Wheel sliders are designed for maneuvering your fifth wheel trailer around sharp corners or in a campground.

Round Tube Slider
  • For use with short box pick-ups; prevents damage to cab when spotting trailer
  • 12" travel front to back
  • Round tube design allows for smoother operation
  • Operation from either side of unit
  • Angled steel rollers reduces binding
  • Operates even when vehicle not in straight line. Operation up to 45° angle.
  • Lock Pin for additional security
  • Rubber grip handle for comfortable operation
  • Tapered holes for easier pull pin engagement
  • 3" wide attachment allows for use with other fifth wheel hitches
  • Uses existing base rail design
  • Minimum 12" extended trailer pin box
  • Rails and installation kit sold separately
Square Tube Slider
    Designed for short bed trucks and extended cab short bed pickups to provide additional turning clearance and to prevent damage to truck when spotting trailer. Fits both 15K and 16K model Fifth Wheel Hitches.
    • 6 roller design
    • 10" travel front to back
    • Works with full size trucks
    • Fully adjustable with trailer attached
    • Minimum 12" extended trailer pin box recommended
    • Requires #30035 Universal Base Rail Kit (sold separately)

 

20K Fifth Wheel
Similar to the 15k & 16k fifth wheel, this unit offers higher weight capacity. Features side-to-side pivot to better accommodate hook-ups on uneven ground. Quick installation, strong, reliable performance and easy removal to allow full truck bed access.

  • Two-jaw locking system with locking jaw handle
  • Fore-and-aft and side-to-side pivot
  • Vertically adjusts from 14-1.4" to 18-1.4"
  • 20,000 lbs. towing capacity/5,000 lbs. pin weight capacity
  • Solid base assembly for superior stability
  • 5 year limited warranty

Signature Series
The ultimate system in fifth wheel towing.

    Towing has never been easier when using the Signature Series system. The Signature Series is the ultimate heavy duty towing platform for hauling fifth wheel or gooseneck trailers. This system has been designed to allow the use of Signature Series Fifth Wheels or Gooseneck products.

    The Signature Series Fifth Wheels have the features that consumers have always asked for. From the large stamped round head to the wider footprint these Fifth Wheels have all the extras designed into them.

    The Signature Series Rail Kit ties your truck and the Fifth Wheel or Gooseneck together. This rail kit is vehicle specific, so you are ensured of a perfect fit every time. The unique feature of the rail kit is the "Power Puck" system, which provides easy installation and securing of the Signature Series Fifth Wheel or Gooseneck. This rail kit provides full use of the truck bed when Fifth Wheel or Gooseneck is dismounted.
